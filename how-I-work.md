# How I work

## Good to know

I prefer to work remote-first and async first. Check the resources at the end of this page to learn more about remote and async work.

I generally work from 09:00 to 19:00 in the local time zone where I am working from. This is usually CET or CET-1.

??? I do adjust to each project's needs regarding onsit

My mobile is usually muted, i.e. fully silenced to not distract me from the thing I’m currently doing. Calling me without an appointment usually doesn’t work. I don't have voicemail.

## On "daily status meetings":

* Daily status meetings are a manifestation of micro-management sold as "good for us™"
* Daily status meetings are a waste of everyone's time and focus


* Find out what needs to be done
* Build a backlog, a roadmap and create a task board
* Each one keeps his tasks updated: the status & and meaningful comments
* Blockers?




* Clarify my role in the project: how can I help?
* Clarify who are the stakeholders of my work.
* Agree on how to measure progress (the distance-to-goal metric)
* Agree on how to make my progress transparent
* Quick-wins & prototypes as the way to go forward in an uncertain project environment
